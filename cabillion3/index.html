Man made forest

üìç Tourist attraction in Loboc, Bohol
Nov 15, 2025

The Bilar Man-Made Forest in Bohol is a dense, 2-kilometer-long forest of thousands of mahogany trees planted in a reforestation project between the towns of Loboc and Bilar. The uniform, straight-lined planting creates a unique, symmetrical, and tunnel-like canopy, making it a popular and cool tourist spot for photos and short nature walks.

üí≠ My Reflection

The Bilar Man-Made Forest in Bohol is a two-kilometer stretch of dense mahogany trees, famous for its stunning tunnel-like views and cool, shaded atmosphere, serving as a popular photo stop and testament to a successful reforestation project started decades ago by planting trees along the Loay Interior Road between Loboc and Bilar towns. It offers a refreshing break from the heat and a chance for reflection on nature restoration, though discussions exist regarding its limited biodiversity compared to native forests, making it a symbol of both human effort and ongoing ecological considerations.

Chocolate Hills

üìç Chocolate Hills, Bohol
Jun 2024

The Chocolate Hills in Bohol, Philippines, are hundreds of uniformly cone-shaped limestone mounds that turn a rich brown during the dry season, resembling chocolate kisses, hence their name, with lush green grass covering them in the wet season. This unique karst landscape, formed by erosion of uplifted coral reefs over millions of years, features over 1,200 symmetrical hills, creating a surreal, iconic tourist attraction with panoramic viewpoints.

üí≠ My Reflection

Visiting the Chocolate Hills in Bohol is a surreal experience, feeling like stepping onto another planet with its thousands of perfectly conical mounds, a testament to nature's unique artistry, transforming from green to iconic brown during dry seasons, reminding us of geological processes, coral reefs uplifted and weathered, a humbling display of Earth's ancient history and constant change, making them a visually stunning, must-see wonder that inspires awe and reflection on time and landscape.
